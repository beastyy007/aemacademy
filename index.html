About AEM

AEM is an academy to prepare and train health care providers for Emergency Medical Service. We also provide a fundamental education in emergency management.
Academy is designed to give basic concepts and planning steps to those new to the field of Emergency so that they may apply planning discipline and skills to challenges in their jobs.
